# Universal Scheduler for Coaches — Product Overview

## The Problem We Solved
Coaches were spending hours manually coordinating school visits, tracking planned dates, managing visit times, and keeping track of which observations were completed. All this manual work meant less time actually coaching.

## What Coaches Can Now Do

### 1. **Smart Plan Tab**
- See a prioritized list of teachers they need to visit
- Quick action to schedule visits with just a few clicks
- Teachers ranked by coaching priority

### 2. **Schedule Visits in Seconds**
When scheduling a visit, coaches fill in:
- **Visit Type** (FICO, Head-Co Observation, M&H, General Visit, RM Visit)
- **Planned Date** (when they're planning to go)
- **Visit Date** (the actual school visit date)
- **Arrival & Departure Times** (what time they'll be there)
- **Week** (optional, for their own organization)

That's it. No complex forms, no back-and-forth.

### 3. **Visits Dashboard**
All visits organized by status:
- **Scheduled Tab** — upcoming visits ready to go (shows teacher, school, visit type, times)
- **Draft Tab** — visits saved as draft to work on later
- **Completed Tab** — finished visits with Neo feedback scores

Each visit card shows:
- Teacher name & school
- Visit type, subject, grade
- Planned date and actual visit date
- Arrival/departure times

### 4. **Full Control Over Visit Lifecycle**
On every visit card, coaches can:
- **Feedback** — record verbal coaching feedback using Neo AI (optional)
- **Draft** — save as draft to continue later
- **Complete** — mark as complete without feedback
- **Delete** — remove if plans change

### 5. **Optional Neo Debrief**
If a coach wants to give real-time feedback to the teacher during/after the visit:
- Click the small mic icon on any visit card
- Record verbal feedback
- Neo analyzes coaching communication quality
- Get instant feedback insights

Neo is optional — coaches can complete visits without it.

## The Flow
1. **Coach opens Smart Plan** → sees teachers ranked by priority
2. **Clicks "Schedule Visit"** on a teacher → fills in visit details (30 seconds)
3. **Gets taken to Visits dashboard** → sees their new visit in Scheduled tab
4. **Visit day arrives** → opens visit card
5. **Can give feedback** (optional mic button) or just mark complete
6. **All visits tracked** → no manual spreadsheets or emails needed

## Key Benefits
✅ **Unified view** — all visits in one place (no scattered emails or notes)
✅ **No manual work** — coaches stop doing admin tasks
✅ **Flexible** — coaches control when/how to complete visits
✅ **Optional AI feedback** — coaches can give feedback if they want, skip if they don't
✅ **Organized by region** — coaches only see their coaches' visits
