UNIT 6.1: CLOSING THE LOOP
Focus: Bridging the gap between the Learn and Improve phases.
Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs):
1. In the Pakistan school context, a coaching cycle "leaks" when: A) The coach forgets to visit. B) A great conversation happens but nothing changes in the classroom. C) The students are absent. D) The school building is damaged.
2. When "Closing the Loop," the coach’s identity shifts from "Inspector" to: A) Evaluator. B) Co-Pilot looking for progress. C) Subject Matter Expert. D) Administrative Assistant.
3. Closing the Loop is the bridge between which two phases of the Impact Cycle? A) Identify and Learn. B) Learn and Improve. C) Improve and Identify. D) Planning and Audit.
4. Which is a "Curious Opener" designed to replace judgmental statements? A) "You didn't use the transition we planned." B) "Why is the class still noisy?" C) "I noticed the 'Think-Pair-Share' didn't happen; what barrier got in the way?" D) "The Principal will be unhappy with this lesson."
5. A "Strong Action Step" in the follow-up protocol must be: A) Broad and complex. B) Bite-sized, observable, and teacher-chosen. C) Determined solely by the coach. D) Based on the annual appraisal form.
6. The purpose of returning to the classroom to "Close the Loop" is to: A) Find faults. B) See if the strategy is working and help the teacher "Pivot" if needed. C) Fill out the final report for the District. D) Give the students a test.
